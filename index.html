Poems for Lea

Eternal Sunshine

In your eyes, I see the dawn of endless days,

Where love's light never fades or sways.

Lea, you are my eternal sunshine,

With you, everything is perfectly fine.


Whispers of the Heart

Every whisper of the heart echoes your name,

In a symphony of love that burns like a flame.

Leanora, my love for you is pure and true,

Forever and always, I belong to you.
